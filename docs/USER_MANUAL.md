# User Manual
**Lohithadharma Lead Scoring Dashboard**

Welcome to the Lead Scoring Dashboard. This guide will walk you through the core features of the system, helping you manage leads, track follow-ups, and generate reports.

---

## 1. Navigating the Dashboard

Upon logging in, you will be greeted by the **Executive Dashboard**:
- **Key Metrics**: View Total Revenue, Active Leads, Conversion Rate, and Average Lead Score at a glance.
- **AI Analytics**: Browse real-time summaries generated by the scoring engine, highlighting where sales focus should be directed.
- **Recent Activities**: A live timeline displaying agent updates, lead registrations, and follow-up activities.

---

## 2. Lead Management & Scoring

Leads represent potential property and plot buyers.
- **Lead Score Indicators**: The platform automatically calculates lead value between `0` and `100` and categorizes them:
  - <span style="color:#ef4444; font-weight:bold;">HOT</span> (>70): Immediate sales priority.
  - <span style="color:#f59e0b; font-weight:bold;">WARM</span> (40-70): Active follow-up recommended.
  - <span style="color:#3b82f6; font-weight:bold;">COLD</span> (<40): Long-term email nurturing.
- **AI Overview**: Open a lead to review the automated **AI Overview** summarizing buyer intent, potential risks (e.g. budget mismatch), and recommended contact channels.

---

## 3. Kanban Pipeline Board

Navigate to **Pipeline** in the sidebar:
- **Visual Stages**: Drag-and-drop leads across stages: *New Lead*, *Contacted*, *Meeting Scheduled*, *Proposal Sent*, *Negotiation*, and *Won/Lost*.
- **Quick Actions**: Double-click a card to view detailed notes or schedule an immediate follow-up task.

---

## 4. Executive Reporting Suite

Managers and Admins can export PDF/Excel/CSV data:
1. Navigate to the **Reports** page.
2. Select your **Report Type** (e.g. Lead Status Report, Revenue Forecast Report, Agent Performance).
3. Select the desired **Date Range** and **Export Format** (PDF, Excel, or CSV).
4. Click **Generate Report**.
5. To schedule automated updates: Click **Schedule Report**, enter recipient email addresses, choose a frequency (Daily, Weekly, Monthly), and click Save.

---

## 5. Notification Center

The bell icon in the top header handles real-time notifications:
- **Alert triggers**: Receive notifications for *New Lead Assigned*, *Follow-up Due*, *Lead Stage Updated*, and *Revenue Achievements*.
- **Unread Count**: The red badge shows your pending notifications. Click on any notification to navigate directly to the lead profile.
- **Mark as Read**: Click individual notifications to dismiss them, or click "Mark all as read" in the dropdown list.
